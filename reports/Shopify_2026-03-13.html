  • Ensure 90+ day stock coverage on Dried Cabbage (26 lb), Black Beans (25 lbs), Dried Peppers Mixed (25 lbs)
  • Negotiate volume pricing with suppliers on these 5–8 anchor products
  • Set automated reorder triggers — no manual intervention
  • Feature these prominently on homepage, category pages, and paid ads

These products are growing despite the broader business decline — they represent the clearest signal of where customer demand is shifting. Invest ahead of the curve here.

✦ Bundle & Cross-Sell Around Top SKUs

Your top SKUs are bulk/wholesale dry goods — the natural extension is meal-kit style bundles that increase average order value without adding SKU complexity.

  • Create a "Soup Foundation Bundle": Cabbage + Carrots + Soup Mix + Onions
  • Create a "Bean Variety Pack": Black Beans + Great Northern + Kidney Beans
  • Upsell sampler packs (Deluxe Sampler, Backpacking Kit) as add-ons at checkout
  • Cross-sell fruit SKUs to vegetable buyers and vice versa
  • Offer subscription/auto-reorder on bulk staples (beans, cabbage, soup mix)

✦ Rationalize: SKU Reduction Candidates

You have 418 active SKUs. Revenue is concentrated in the top 30. The long tail consumes inventory capital, warehouse space, catalog attention, and ops bandwidth for minimal return. Systematic reduction frees resources for winners.

  • Any SKU generating less than $500 in 2025 with a declining trajectory: candidate for removal
  • SKUs that completely disappeared in 2025 from 2024 top 30 — audit for stockout vs. discontinue decision
  • Target a focused catalog of 40–50 core SKUs for 2026 planning
  • Liquidate slow-moving inventory through bundle discounts or bulk deals rather than holding
  • Redirect catalog page authority (SEO) from low-traffic pages to top 20 product pages

✦ Revenue Recovery Target

The business peaked at $1.81M in 2021 and is now at $1.02M in 2025 — a 43% decline over 4 years. A realistic 2026 recovery strategy:

  • Defend base: Prevent further decline in top 10 SKUs (protect $400K+ in anchor revenue)
  • Grow emerging: Lean into Dried Corn, Potato Starch, Dark Kidney Beans — 3 SKUs with breakout potential
  • Revive 1–2 fallen anchors: Even recovering Cabbage or Carrots 50% back toward 2024 = +$17K–$30K
  • Add bundles: If top 5 SKUs increase AOV by 20% through bundles, that's ~+$28K alone
  • Reduce tail: Cutting 100+ low-performing SKUs saves ops cost without meaningful revenue loss
